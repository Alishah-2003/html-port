The Best Movies Accoring To Me!

My top THREE movies of all time :

Jab We Met

The characters and chemistry makes this a beautiful movie for those who love to watch rom-coms!

Me Before You

A perfect movie with many heart-touching elements, but unfortunately a tragic ending which breaks our hearts.

Kal Ho Na Ho

Just as the name suggests, the movie really portraits the strong thought of whether or not there would a tomorrow. This amazing movie showcased brilliant acting, alongside with heartfelt and comedy elements which didn't fail to capture our hearts!
